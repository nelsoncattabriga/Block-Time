FD14.3 — Where >2 duty periods involve late night ops in any 7 consecutive night period:
(a) Max 40 hrs duty in that 7-night period.
(b) Max 4 duty periods in that 7-night period (except per FD14.2).
(c) After consecutive late nights: remain on late night ops (up to FD14.1/14.2 limits) and/or min 24 hrs free of duty before any other duty.

FD14.4 — Back of clock operation (≥2 hrs between 0100–0459 local at departure): next duty in Australia sign-on no earlier than 1000 local the following day.
Deadheading Following a Flight Duty FD15
FD15.1 — May deadhead following flight duty up to Operational Limits (FD23.1 table).
FD15.2 — At pilot discretion, FD23.1 limits may be extended when deadheading.
FD15.3 — Deadheading included in total duty period for rest and FDT calculations.
FD15.4 — Deadheading sector (if last sector) does not count in sector determination for max duty.
FD15.5 — Deadheading sector before a flight duty is counted as a sector.
FD15.6 — No duty period with flight duty to exceed 16 hours.
