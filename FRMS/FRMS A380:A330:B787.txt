Based on the FRMS PDF for A380/A330/B787, here are all the flight and duty limits that need to be tracked:

  2 PILOT OPERATIONS

  Duty Period Limits (by sign-on time):

  - 0500-0759: 11 hours
  - 0800-1359: 11 hours (12 hours day pattern only)
  - 1400-1559: 11 hours
  - 1600-0459: 10 hours

  Flight Time Limits:

  - 0500-0759: 8 hours
  - 0800-1359: 8.5 hours (9.5 hours day pattern only)
  - 1400-1559: 8.5 hours
  - 1600-0459: 8 hours
  - Operational: 9.5-10.5 hours depending on conditions

  Sector Limits:

  - 1 sector if any sector flight time > 6 hours
  - 2-4 sectors depending on time and conditions

  Rest Requirements:

  - Pre-duty rest: 11-22 hours depending on duty period
  - Post-duty rest: 11-22 hours depending on duty period
  - Within 7 days: 1 continuous period embracing 2200-0600 on 2 consecutive nights

  ---
  3 PILOT OPERATIONS

  Duty Period Limits:

  - CLASS 2 REST: 12 hours (planning), 16 hours (operational)
  - CLASS 1 REST: 14 hours (planning), 18 hours (operational)

  Flight Time Limits:

  - CLASS 2 REST: 8.5 hours
  - CLASS 1 REST: 12.5 hours
  - Operational: 8 consecutive hours active duty, max 14 hours total duty in flight deck

  Sector Limits:

  - 3 sectors if duty period > 11 hours, otherwise max 4 sectors
  - < 2 sectors if duty period scheduled to exceed 14 hours (operational)

  Rest Requirements:

  - Pre-duty: 10-12 hours
  - Post-duty: 12-32 hours depending on duty period and acclimation

  ---
  4 PILOT OPERATIONS

  Duty Period Limits:

  - 2 x CLASS 2 REST: 16 hours
  - 1 x CLASS 1 & 1 x CLASS 2 REST: 17.5 hours (planning), 20 hours (operational)
  - 2 x CLASS 1 REST: 20 hours (planning), 21 hours (operational for >18 hour duties)

  Flight Time Limits:

  - A pilot cannot spend more than 8 continuous hours on duty in flight deck
  - No more than 14 hours total duty in flight deck

  Sector Limits:

  - < 2 rostered sectors if duty period scheduled to exceed 14 hours
  - 1 rostered sector if duty period scheduled to exceed 16 hours

  Rest Requirements:

  - Pre-duty: 12-48 hours depending on duty period length
  - Post-duty: 12-48 hours depending on duty period length and conditions
  - Special rules for >18 hour duties: 22-36 hours depending on crew composition

  ---
  CUMULATIVE LIMITS (ALL OPERATIONS)

  Flight Time:

  - 7 consecutive days (2 pilot): 30 hours
  - 7 consecutive days (mixed 2/3+ pilot): 30-40 hours depending on next flight
  - 30 consecutive days: 100 hours
  - 365 consecutive days: 900 hours

  Duty Time:

  - 7 consecutive days: 60 hours
  - 14 consecutive days: 100 hours
  - Following 50 hours of duty: 24 hours rest before next duty

  Fortnight Limits (2 pilot operational):

  - 90 hours total duty per fortnight (includes standby, deadhead, admin, training)

  ---
  DEADHEADING

  Duty Period Limits:

  - Solely deadhead: 26 hours (max 2 sectors with first class)
  - Operate then deadhead (not to base): 14.5 hours
  - Operate then deadhead (to base/posting): 18 hours

  Rest Requirements:

  - Pre-duty: 11-18 hours depending on duty period
  - Post-duty: 11-18 hours depending on duty period

  ---
  STANDBY DUTY

  - Duration: Maximum 12 consecutive hours
  - Location: Must have access to suitable sleeping accommodation

  ---
  SPECIAL OPERATIONS (>18 HOURS)

  Routes (A380 & B787 only):

  - Sydney-Dallas, Melbourne-Dallas, Perth-London, Auckland-New York

  Crew Requirements:

  - Minimum 4 pilots

  Duty Period Limit:

  - 21 hours operational

  Rest Requirements (Disruptions):

  - Prior to Relevant Sector: 22 hours
  - After Relevant Sector (Captain OR First Officer): 27 hours
  - After Relevant Sector (Captain OR F/O, duty >20 hrs): 36 hours
  - After Relevant Sector (Captain AND First Officer): 36 hours
  - After inbound to Australia/NZ (same timezone return): 36 hours
  - After inbound to Australia/NZ (domestic/trans-Tasman): 22 hours

  Additional MBTT:

  - Increased by 1 local night after patterns containing planned duty >18 hours

  ---
  MINIMUM BASE TURNAROUND TIME (MBTT)

  - 1 day away: 12 hours
  - 2-4 days away: 1 local night
  - 5-8 days away: 2 local nights
  - 9-12 days away: 3 local nights
  - >20 credited flight hours: 2 local nights
  - >40 credited flight hours: 3 local nights
  - >60 credited flight hours or >12 days away: 4 local nights
  - Excess over 100 hours in 30 days: 1-2 additional local nights

  This is a comprehensive list of all limits that need to be tracked for A380/A330/B787 FRMS compliance.
