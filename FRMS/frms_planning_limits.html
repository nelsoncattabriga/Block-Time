Patterns > 18 Hours — Relevant Sectors (A380 & B787 Only) — FD3.4
Relevant Sectors
a) Any planned duty period greater than 18 hours
b) Sydney to Dallas and vice versa
c) Melbourne to Dallas and vice versa
d) Perth to London and vice versa
e) Auckland to New York and vice versa
FD3.4.1 — Crew complement: minimum of four (4) pilots.
FD3.4.2 — Following any pattern that includes a planned duty > 18 hours, the MBTT in FD9 will be increased by 1 local night.
FD3.4.3 — A pilot who operates a pattern that includes a planned duty > 18 hours will be provided with home transport.
